About me

Hi, my name is Rikke Juliane!

I’m on a journey to embrace change, both in my personal life and through my professional growth. As someone who used to live with severe agoraphobia, I’ve learned to navigate life within the limits of my comfort zone, but I’m determined to keep pushing those boundaries.

In 2024, I took a significant step by starting my coding journey at Noroff University. Coding has become not just a skill, but a path to building something meaningful, whether it’s for personal projects, helping others, or shaping my future career. I’m working toward building my confidence, gaining more independence, and pursuing a life that’s more than just existing—it’s about truly thriving.

One of the greatest joys in my life is being a proud owner of Fie, my Maltipoo. She’s not just a pet; she’s my partner in this journey. Her playful energy and loyalty have been a constant source of comfort, reminding me that small moments of happiness are key to building confidence and resilience.
